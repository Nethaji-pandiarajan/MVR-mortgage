Our Team

With over 5 years and thousands of loans of experience, our team is excited to fulfill your mortgage needs!

Venkat Malapati

Whether you’re buying, selling, refinancing, or building your dream home, you have a lot riding on your loan officer. When I bought my first home in 2011, I was astounded by the complexity of getting a simple home loan. With no one to advise me, I ended up choosing the loan option that was marketed the best, not the one that fit my and my family’s lifestyle.

Years later, when I was introduced to Mortgage Loan Origination, I decided to help people avoid my mistakes. Since then, I’ve won multiple awards for my work, including being in the Top 10 Mortgage Brokers by Expertise.com, being ranked among the top 1% brokers nationally, and one of the fastest closers. And yes, I’ve refinanced that home loan to fit my life better. I have the knowledge, expertise, and care you need to explore the many financing options available. As a Certified Mortgage Advisor, I am committed to advising my customers with mortgage services that exceed their expectations and prepare them to purchase multiple properties.

I know that buying a home is a stressful and time-consuming process, so I’ve posted some of the decision-making tools and calculators I use daily to this website. As you browse this website, check out the different loan programs we have available and use our secure online application to get started. After you’ve applied, I’ll call you to discuss your loan details, or you can set up an appointment with me using my online form.

As always, you can contact me anytime by phone, fax, or email for personalized service and expert advice. I look forward to working with you.
